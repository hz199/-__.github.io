flutter 启动页黑屏/白屏解决方法

  • flutter

# 前言

最近在学习flutter中，突然发现app在启动的时候会先出现白屏，然后接着出现一段黑屏，最后才会出现页面，这样感觉很丑，观察了微信，钉钉的app启动发现没有这个过程，最后上网搜了下解决办法. 在这里记录一下

# 安卓的配置

  • 先看完整代码 android > app > src > main > AndroidManifest.xml
<manifest xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android"
    package="com.example.flutterwidgetdemo">
    <!-- io.flutter.app.FlutterApplication is an android.app.Application that
         calls FlutterMain.startInitialization(this); in its onCreate method.
         In most cases you can leave this as-is, but you if you want to provide
         additional functionality it is fine to subclass or reimplement
         FlutterApplication and put your custom class here. -->
    <application
        android:name="io.flutter.app.FlutterApplication"
        android:label="flutterwidgetdemo"
        android:icon="@mipmap/ic_launcher">
        <activity
            android:name=".MainActivity"
            android:launchMode="singleTop"
            android:theme="@style/LaunchTheme"
            android:configChanges="orientation|keyboardHidden|keyboard|screenSize|smallestScreenSize|locale|layoutDirection|fontScale|screenLayout|density|uiMode"
            android:hardwareAccelerated="true"
            android:windowSoftInputMode="adjustResize">
            <intent-filter>
                <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN"/>
                <category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER"/>
            </intent-filter>
            
            <!-- qpp 启动页配置 -->
            <meta-data
                android:name="io.flutter.app.android.SplashScreenUntilFirstFrame"
                android:value="true"/>

            <!-- flutter 启动 -->
            <meta-data
                android:name="io.flutter.embedding.android.SplashScreenDrawable"
                android:resource="@drawable/launch_background" />
        </activity>
        <!-- Don't delete the meta-data below.
             This is used by the Flutter tool to generate GeneratedPluginRegistrant.java -->
        <meta-data
            android:name="flutterEmbedding"
            android:value="2" />
    </application>
</manifest>
  • 代码中添加 下方代码 value 为true 代码app存在启动页
<meta-data
  android:name="io.flutter.app.android.SplashScreenUntilFirstFrame"
  android:value="true"/>

打开res > values > style.xml 启动页的背景配置文件存在放 drawable/launch_background

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<resources>
    <style name="LaunchTheme" parent="@android:style/Theme.Black.NoTitleBar">
        <!-- Show a splash screen on the activity. Automatically removed when
             Flutter draws its first frame -->
        <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/launch_background</item>
    </style>
</resources>

打开 res > drawable > launch_background.xml

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<!-- Modify this file to customize your launch splash screen -->
<layer-list xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <!-- <item android:drawable="@android:color/white" /> --> <!-- 这个是启动页的背景颜色-->
    <item android:drawable="@color/MyColor" /> <!-- 这个是自定义的背景颜色可以修改 -->

    <!-- You can insert your own image assets here -->
    <item>
        <bitmap
            android:gravity="center"
            android:src="@drawable/splash" /> <!-- 图片的路径 -->
    </item>
</layer-list>
  • 上面说到启动页可以修改背景颜色，只需要在values文件夹创建 colors.xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<resources>
    <color name="MyColor">#FFAB40</color>
    <color name="transparent">#00000000</color>
</resources>
